Hungry?

We Are...

a Tex Mex/American style taco shop and bar located in a small borough called Bellevue, right on the rim of the Pittsburgh city area. With Bellevue being a tight-knit community this is the perfect place to grab lunch, dinner, a drink, to watch your favoite sports teams or even host a party. Being partnered with other local businesses on a few of our menu items lets you know that we're not only here to feed the community but to be a strong part of it.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be that fun & tasty place that continues to be a driving staple for Bellevue. What we want to do is bring people together in a way that everyone loves - eating (tacos of course), drinking, hanging out and occasionally doing some karoke! We want to prove that in a foodie city like Pittsburgh, small businesses can make the most difference.

Chow Down

Eat With Us

With our variety of tacos, chips & dips, mexican street corn & desserts, we're sure you'll find something on the menu that you'll fall in love with. Whether you're vegan, a meat lover or in a seafood kind of mood, we have a taco for you! In addition, we also have rice bowls & quesadillas, with the Bellevue Bowl being our signature bowl. If you're not in the mood for any any of our featured menu items we also have the option of building your own tacos, bowls or quesadillas. And don't get me started on our small menu of desserts...delicioso.


Pour Up

Drink With Us

Stocked with a full bar of liquor, beers that are both domestic and imported, over 10 flavors of our in-house margaritas (also available to go), 202 Tacos is sure to have what you're looking to drink. Don't hesistate to stop in for our Happy Hour 4pm-6pm Monday-Friday.
